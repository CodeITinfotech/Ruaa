About us

VISHLIST

Welcome to Ruaa, where style meets sophistication and every fit tells a story. We are more than just a fashion brand; we are a celebration of individuality, confidence and the timeless elegance that resides within every woman.

Ruaa curates a collection that redefines the narrative of female clothing, offering a diverse range that caters to the multifaceted personalities and lifestyles of modern women. From casual chic Indo-western wear to our beautiful Pakistani Collection, our range of clothing is as diverse as the women who wear them.

At the heart of our brand is a commitment to quality craftsmanship and attention to detail. We are proud to have served 50,000+ customers with our design solutions! Our team is dedicated to delivering quality work and outstanding customer service.

THE FOUNDER

With the passion to empower women through fashion, Mavish Banducda found Ruaa at the tender age of 21. With dreams in her eyes, love for fashion in her heart and a determined mind, she began her entrepreneurial journey all by herself.

Mavish, a simple cheerful girl from Goa, aims to break the cycle of overpriced designer outfits, giving every woman her dream outfit at budget friendly prices.

Join us in this journey of celebrating the beautiful individual that you are.
